## Speaking Topics

<div class="topic-grid">
    <div class="topic-card">

This keynote introduces systematic frameworks for translating geopolitical signals, policy drafts, and incentive structures into early warning systems—enabling action while decision margin still exists.

        <p class="topic-description">

This talk explores how to protect relational intelligence while AI accelerates operational tempo—addressing the practical challenges leaders face when half their team doesn't need feedback, inspiration, or career development.
        </p>
        <div class="topic-audience">
            <strong>Ideal for:</strong> Leadership conferences, technology summits, management workshops
        </div>

    <div class="topic-card">
        <div class="topic-label">Federal Compliance</div>
        <h3>Policy Translation: Reading Regulatory Intent Before Final Publication</h3>
        <p class="topic-description">
            Organizations that wait for final CMMC, FedRAMP, or NIST guidance face compressed timelines and resource constraints. This workshop demonstrates systematic methods for reading regulatory intent in draft comment periods, legislative signals, and agency coordination patterns—providing months of preparation advantage.
        </p>
        <div class="topic-audience">
            <strong>Ideal for:</strong> Defense contractor conferences, federal compliance summits, GRC workshops
        </div>
    </div>

    <div class="topic-card">
        <div class="topic-label">Game Theory</div>
        <h3>Incentive Analysis for Control Design: Why Audit Findings Predict Less Than You Think</h3>
        <p class="topic-description">
            Audit findings emerge from auditor incentive structures, not just technical gaps. This presentation applies game theory to GRC program design, demonstrating how stakeholder incentive mapping (auditors, engineers, executives) creates controls that survive audit pressure while maintaining operational velocity.
        </p>
        <div class="topic-audience">
            <strong>Ideal for:</strong> Security operations conferences, compliance workshops, risk management summits
        </div>
